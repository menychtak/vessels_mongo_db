**# NOAA Weather Data**

## Dataset Overview
This repository contains NOAA weather data from the years 2017, 2018, and 2019. The dataset includes various weather attributes in shapefile format.

## Download Data
Download and extract the dataset from the following link:

[NOAA Weather Data](https://zenodo.org/records/6323416/files/noaa_weather.zip?download=1)

## Folder Structure
After extracting the data, the folder structure should be as follows:

```
noaa_weather/
├── 2017/
│   ├── aug/
│   ├── dec/
│   ├── jul/
│   ├── jun/
│   ├── may/
│   ├── nov/
│   ├── oct/
│   ├── sep/
│
├── 2018/
│   ├── apr/
│   ├── aug/
│   ├── dec/
│   ├── feb/
│   ├── jan/
│   ├── jul/
│   ├── jun/
│   ├── mar/
│   ├── may/
│   ├── nov/
│   ├── oct/
│   ├── sep/
│
├── 2019/
│   ├── apr/
│   ├── aug/
│   ├── dec/
│   ├── feb/
│   ├── jan/
│   ├── jul/
│   ├── jun/
│   ├── mar/
│   ├── may/
│   ├── nov/
│   ├── oct/
│   ├── sep/
```
Each subfolder contains multiple shapefiles (`.cpg`, `.dbf`, `.prj`, `.shp`, `.shx`) corresponding to that month's weather data.
